# 🚗 Car Sales & Inventory Analysis (Power BI)

## 🔹 Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze car sales data, inventory composition, and technical specifications. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of vehicle trends, pricing insights, and market distribution.

---

## 🔹 Objectives

* Analyze total inventory and pricing trends
* Identify top-performing car models
* Understand seller distribution (Dealer vs Individual)
* Explore transmission and ownership patterns
* Track fuel type trends over time

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* Microsoft Excel (Dataset)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Inventory: **301 Cars**
* Total Selling Price: **1.40K**
* Total Present Price: **2.30K**

---

## 🔹 Key Analysis Areas

### 📊 Sales & Inventory Distribution

* Top models include **Fortuner**, **City**, **Innova**, and **Corolla Altis**
* Dealers dominate the market with ~65% share
* Individuals contribute ~35% of listings

### ⚙️ Transmission & Ownership

* Manual cars dominate (**86.7%**)
* Automatic cars form a smaller segment
* Majority vehicles are **first-owner (~96%)**

### ⛽ Fuel Type Trends

* Petrol cars show significant growth after 2010
* Diesel maintains steady presence
* CNG has minimal contribution
